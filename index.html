ABOUT

City Sports & F1

Welcome to City Sports & F1, your very own hub for a wide range of sports merchandise. As an official retailer, we have a huge AFL collection, as well as Formula One, soccer, rugby, cricket and tennis merchandise from across the globe. For sports fans and collectors alike, this is your one stop shop for sports merchandise.

We are an independent, family-run team who are always looking to help you find what it is you are after. If you are on the hunt for a piece of merchandise that we don't seem to have, simply get in touch with us by phone on 0396545448, or e-mail on info@citysportsandf1.com.au. Our friendly sales staff will be more than happy to help you out.

Formula 1 Merchandise

We stock the latest merchandise from all your favourite teams and drivers. If you are a fan of an F1 team, then you will be able to browse through specific collections such McLaren, AMG Petronas, Red Bull Racing, Scuderia Ferrari, BMW Motorsport, Renault and more. We also stock a wide variety of merchandise from the Australian Formula 1 Grand Prix Event. Our F1 grand prix collection includes keyrings, event caps and t shirts for men, women and children.

AFL Merchandise

This is also the home for all your AFL merchandise: we always have the latest merchandise from every team in the league. With guernseys, hoodies, jackets, polos and more, there's so much high quality official apparel to get your hands on. On top of the wide range of clothing, you will also see that we have bottles, balls, luggage, keyrings and many more accessories. Not only do we have all the latest stock, we also have an extensive AFL clearance range - something for every team.

Australian Open Merchandise

The Australian Open is one of the biggest sporting events in the calendar, not only in the southern hemisphere but in the whole world. If you are looking for official AO merch, then you are in luck. We stock official AO range - the same merch available at Melbourne park, and so we have all the latest branded t shirts, bottles, caps, towels and other accessories. Whether you want something to play in, to chill out in, or you are simply adding some merch to the collection, you will find lots here to be excited about.

Soccer, Rugby, Cricket & MotoGP Merchandise

But this is not even scratching the surface of our range, as we also have lots to explore in other sports such as Soccer, Rugby, Cricket and MotoGP. In terms of soccer, we have merch from all the biggest international teams such as Manchester United, Barcelona, Liverpool and Juventus, but then we also have a great collection of Aussie soccer merchandise.

This goes for our rugby collection too. We have plenty of Wallabies, All Blacks, Melbourne Storm and NSW and QLD State of Origin merchandise: there'll be something for you whoever you follow. Don't forget about our extensive cricket range including the Cricket Australia range and all the Big Bash League teams. We also have plenty of teamwear and Grand Prix Event merch in our Moto GP collection that can't be missed.
